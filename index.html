Amadasoft

Amada Soft India belongs to Amada Group, a pioneer of sheet metal automation solutions. Amada Soft India, located at IIT M Research Park, Chennai is the core R&D center for Amada group

Built with ease.

AMADA provides "comprehensive solutions” for sheet metal industry, through integrated, network intelligent CNC machines, software products and services to manufacture sheet metal parts for industry segments like infrastructure, telecom, electronics, home/office equipment, retail businesses and gaming consoles, to name a few. Major product lines of Amada include Sheet Metal Processing, Structural Steel Processing, Stamping Press Machinery, and Metal Cutting Machine Tools.

Production Management

In today’s global environment, companies are facing the competitive challenge of analysing and converting engineering data into business-critical information for a faster time-to-market.

Software systems play a major role in this process, as they enable organizations to collect and easily access large volumes of data. AMADA focused this idea into the VPSS (Virtual Prototype Simulation System) concept, which has the final aim of significantly optimizing your productivity.

AMADA provides a complete suite of software products, perfectly integrated, and totally focused on applications for the sheet metal industry. In this document you will find information about all the solution packs related to the different sheet metal applications.

Our software solution packs

Product designer

Production Designer allows you to import different 3D data formats; it automatically converts them into 3D sheetmetal parts and save data into the AMADA Digital Database.

VPSS3i BEND

The next step in off-line bending CAM solutions.Conceived from the actual AMADA bending solution experience, VPSS3i BEND is the perfect match of a very simple all-in-one user interface and high level automatic bending algorithms.

VPSS3i BLANK

The evolution of CAM for AMADA blanking machines. Fully integrated in the VPSS3i suite, VPSS3i BLANK is a perfect mix of advanced algorithms for automatic processing and a smart manual environment for a quick manual editing.

Dr. ABE TUBE

Dr. ABE TUBE provides fully automated off-line programming and nesting for AMADA tube lasers. It is directly linked to AMADA’s 3D modelling system, SheetWorks, for fast switching between CAD and CAM environments.
